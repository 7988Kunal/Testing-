AIML Online Examination Instructions

  • Total Duration of the Examination is 3 Hours.
  • The question paper consists of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
  • Each correct answer: +4 Marks
  • Each incorrect answer: -1 Mark (Negative Marking)
  • No mark will be deducted for unanswered questions.
  • Use the ‘Save & Next’ button to move to the next question after answering.
  • You can ‘Mark for Review’ questions for later before final submission.
  • Question status will be shown in different colors:
    • Not Visited
    • Not Answered
    • Answered
    • Marked for Review
  • You can submit your exam any time but must complete your exam.
